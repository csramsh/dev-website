# Mesh Events

We run regular events to help the community practice, connect, and grow. All events are open to anyone interested in mesh networking!

## Weekly Friday Net

### What is a Net?

A **Net** is a formalized way to test equipment, grow skills, and build connections — adapted from Amateur Radio practices for Meshtastic application.

### Schedule

- **Day:** Every Friday
- **Check-in window:** 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM EST
- Participants record observations via Google Form
- Results are compiled into a shareable sheet and posted to Discord

### How to Check In

During the check-in window, send a single message on the mesh in the following format:

```
[Short Node Name] CSRANet [City] [Router] [# Nodes]
```

**Data collected per check-in:**

1. Node originator identification (short name)
2. General location (city reference)
3. Router with best connection, or "None"
4. Count of active nodes visible in the last hour

**Examples:**

```
Mi57 CSRANet Thomson None 3
Mi57 CSRANet Evans CSR1 34
```

### Submit Your Check-In Report

After checking in on the mesh, fill out the [Net Check-In Report Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdYX_m9PxxaR6tYOQmjhU_uuKD6WxU-0YQ8VRC-Cyn2S5Qd4Q/viewform?usp=publish-editor) to help us track mesh health and coverage.

**Form fields:**

- Your Node Short Name
- Reporting your own or others' check-in
- Date/Time message received
- Complete check-in message text

The net also encourages direct mesh communication between participants — so get on the mesh and say hello!

## Monthly Meet & Mesh

We hold a monthly **in-person gathering** on the **3rd week of the month** at local restaurants in the CSRA area. Open to anyone interested in mesh networking.

### What We Discuss

- Industry trends in mesh and LoRa technology
- Local mesh growth updates and coverage reports
- Knowledge sharing and tips
- Project discussions and show & tell

### Details

Details for each month's Meet & Mesh (location, date, time) are distributed via our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/mgzj2PmhKf) and [Facebook Group](https://www.facebook.com/share/g/15wMGLcU8Q/). Join us to stay in the loop!
